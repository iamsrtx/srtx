My Story


I started out as a computer engineer. My first brush with design happened in my first year of engineering. I started learning Coreldraw and started using it in small design applications. I started learning the favourable fonts as well as various aspects of design thinking. Later, I, along with my friends ended up creating a small design consultancy that went on to acquire a few clients along with my college as the primary one among them. After graduation, I joined 9cubes as a product manager and worked on their web portal as well as product line-up. But the quest to find and develop a good design never stopped. During my MS degree at University of Illinois at Urbana- Champaign, I was part of a group that worked on human comouter interaction and designs. This is where I got properly introduced the term UX or user experience. I started out to make more and more designs, and most of them were either failed or disfunctional designs. Eventually, I learnt the art of developing a good UI as well as a good UX. In all my years of product management, I have been striving for development of good as well as funcitonal designs and applications.

What I Offer!

Over the years, I have added a bunch of skills to my craft. Some of them are:

Web Development

I am a HTML5 and PHP developer who creates scalable web applications with a great design.

Product Management

If you are an entrepreneur, you know that your success depends on finding the right product-market fit. Over the years as a product manager, I have learnt to put users as my top priority. Every metric comes after user satisfaction.

Designing User Experiences

Users are the center of any product you build and great user experiences goes a long way in attracting and keeping users. To take a page from Apple's co-founder Steve Jobs' book,"If user can't use it, it doesn't work".

Mobile App Development

As Matt Galligan, co-founder of Circa, puts it,"In my opinion, the future of mobile is the future of everything". With the explosion of smartphone users in the world, desktop is becoming a passive medium of data consumption. A clean interface and a lean application is all you need to get a user hooked once you have a great product.

Graphic Design

This is where it all started. Coreldraw, Photoshop, Illustrator laid the foundation of my understanding of user interfaces and human computer interaction. I admit some fonts make the whole thing look so much better.

Photography

This is an acquired skill as well as a hobby. It helps me learn perspectives and keeps my mind off stress.
